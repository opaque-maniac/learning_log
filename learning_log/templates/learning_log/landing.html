Welcome to Learning Log, the all round perfect learning progress tracker. It works great for any new field of interest you chose to pursue. This is because it will give you all your learning progress on one platform. You can see the things you did when learning, what you could have done better. You can evaluate your areas of weakness and know what to improve next time. You can also use the logs to help others who might be encountering the same problem since you logged your own solution on the platform.

Log in or Sign up to begin using it. Then, create a new topic by giving it a name. Add entries detailing your learning progress about the specific topic over a period of time. You can also edit entries you wish to change, say if you need to correct a mistake or you feel like they do not correctly describe the learning step. You can also delete the ones you deem irrelevant. Topics can also be deleted once you are done with them or you do not wish to continue storing them and their entries anymore.

We hope that you enjoy using this platform for all things you learn. If you have any queries and complaints, please let us know on our social media platforms. Also, if you happen to have suggestions on how we can improve the platform, please let us know.

Do note however that you cannot see other people's topics and entries and they cannot see yours. Your learning progress is yours and yours alone and cannot be accessed, viewed or modified by a user that is not you.

Happy Logging!
